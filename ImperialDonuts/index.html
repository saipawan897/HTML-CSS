About

Imperial Donuts is an independently owned and family run business that specializes in the creation of artisan donuts.Established in early 2014 , we have been blown away by the unexpected sucess and popularity of our carefully crafted donuts.Our donuts are freshly prepared each day prior to sun-rise and amongst our most popular donuts include the honey-glazed donut , the jam filled donut and our iced-glaze donut.

Currently we make 117 different types of donuts on a daily-basis and have two stores in the California area, one store in Montreal and on store in Chicago.Definitely pop by one of our locations to try atleast one of our donuts but if you can't make it - dont worry! We've recelty introduced catering service for those speical occassions such as weddings &birthday's and have also recently introduced a delivery service for our 'Surprise Donut Collection' box which contains 6 different donut flavours.
